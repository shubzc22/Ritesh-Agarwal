About Me

I’m Ritesh Agarwal, founder & CEO of OYO — a brand born from a simple belief: everyone deserves quality stays at affordable prices.

From starting as a curious teenager with big dreams to building a global hospitality network, my journey has been driven by hustle, vision, and an obsession with solving real problems. I believe in taking bold risks, learning fast, and scaling what works.

This is just the beginning.
